Blaseball: The Musical

Nearly 40 volunteers from around the world organized via Discord to write, record, and edit a digital musical in 72 hours over the course of three weekends, centered around the baseball simulation horror game and internet phenomenon Blaseball. As a production assistant, I maintained the website, edited the script, and tracked, verified, and organized recordings and sheet music, and some transcription.
